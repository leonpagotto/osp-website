## 🎯 Project Overview

### Mission
Transform the OSP website into a comprehensive, modern digital platform that accurately represents OSP's positioning as a strategic accounting partner for companies operating under "Lucro Real" tax regime.

### Goals
- ✅ Set up local development environment
- 📝 Map all pages and content from NEW_WEBSITE folder
- 🏗️ Build component architecture
- 🎨 Implement modern, professional design
- 🚀 Deploy production-ready website

### Target Audience
- Companies in Lucro Real tax regime
- Industrial companies
- Multinational corporations
- Family business groups
- High-complexity service companies
